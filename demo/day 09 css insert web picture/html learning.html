This is a local food in my hometown, and i love it so much.

Delicious Food Legal


Lists of the apparatus and materials

  • Two tomatoes
  • Two eggs
  • A spoon of cooking wine (5 ml)
  • A cup of water (15 ml)
  • Two shallots
  • Two spoon of white sugar (10 g)
  • Two cup of tomato cheese (30 ml)
  • A spoon of salt (3 g)

Steps

  1. Broke the eggs and put into a bowl. Add cooking wine and water into it.Then, mix them.
  2. Burns the pot until it becomes hot. Then, put the mixture of eggs into it and use the shovel to stir it.
  3. Take it out. Then, put the tomato into the pot. Burns the tomato until the skin of tomato become dry.
  4. Take the tomato out and make it into slice, at the same time wash and cut the shallots.
  5. Put the oil into pots again and burn the oil until the oil produce bubbles. Then, put the mixture of egg, tomato and shallots into the pots. After 2 minutes, add the sugar and tomato cheese into the pot.
  6. Finish it!!!
